🎮 GAME SYSTEMS AT A GLANCE

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

⏱️  CLOCK
    └─ Normal: 720 min (12 hours gameplay)
    └─ Hard: 360 min (6 hours gameplay)
    └─ Speedrun: 360 min (race mode)
    └─ Each choice costs time
    └─ Game over when clock hits 0:00

🏆 RANK
    └─ Starts: OFFICER
    └─ +10 points per decision
    └─ +50 points per combo
    └─ Goes up to: ARCHITECT (7 tiers)
    └─ Display: Top-right of screen

🔗 CLUE COMBOS
    └─ Collect 2+ matching clues
    └─ System auto-detects
    └─ Unlocks new scene
    └─ Extra rewards (Insight, Score)
    └─ 20 total combinations possible

📱 MESSAGES
    └─ Radio: Dispatch
    └─ Text: Jonas
    └─ Signal: Mira
    └─ Appear contextually
    └─ Indicator: 📨 X NEW (top-right)

💾 SAVE/LOAD
    └─ Ctrl+S: Quick save to slot
    └─ F1-F8: Quick load from slot
    └─ Ctrl+L: Load selected slot
    └─ 8 slots total
    └─ Auto-saved to %LOCALAPPDATA%

🎯 STATS (6 meters on right side)
    └─ SHIFT (0-7): Reality breakdown
    └─ STRESS (0-7): Mental state
    └─ TRUST (-3→+3): Jonas relationship
    └─ INSIGHT (0-10): Pattern recognition
    └─ ACCESS (0-10): Technical skill
    └─ INTEGRITY (0-10): Identity

🎖️  ACHIEVEMENTS
    └─ Auto-unlock on trigger
    └─ 40+ total badges
    └─ Visible in ACHIEVEMENTS tab
    └─ Some are secret
    └─ Reward score when unlocked
